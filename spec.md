# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Build a professional, single-page static website for "Not Ya Mama's Cooking," a Mexican catering business serving Phoenix, Arizona, with a warm vibrant aesthetic, food showcase, catering section, and contact info.

**Planned changes:**
- Create a single-page React frontend with a warm Mexican-inspired color palette (deep reds, earthy oranges, golden yellows, dark greens), bold typography, and festive upscale design
- Implement a sticky header that hides on scroll-down and reappears on scroll-up with smooth animation
- Add a hamburger menu icon (top-right) that opens a navigation drawer listing all sections (Catering Services, Chicken Street Tacos, Beef Street Tacos, Burritos, Contact); clicking an item scrolls to that section and closes the menu
- Display the logo (logo.dim_400x400.png) prominently in the header/hero area
- Build a hero section with the business name and a tagline
- Build a Menu section with cards for Chicken Street Tacos, Beef Street Tacos, and Burritos, each with a food image and short description; street tacos visually emphasized as the signature item
- Build a Catering Services section featuring the catering-banner image, listing catering offerings, mentioning Phoenix, Arizona, and including a CTA button linking to the contact section
- Build a Contact section displaying "Jasmine Cota" and a clickable tel link for "(480) 091-4039" with a warm inviting message
- Footer with business name and relevant info only — no "Built by Caffeine AI" attribution anywhere on the site

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can browse a visually rich Mexican restaurant/catering website, view the food menu with photos, learn about catering services in the Phoenix area, and easily contact Jasmine Cota via a clickable phone number.
